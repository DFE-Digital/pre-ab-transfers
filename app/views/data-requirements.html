You’ll need the following information about a trainee to complete their record.

Personal details

  • First names
  • Middle names
  • Last or family name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Nationality

Contact details

  • Address
  • Email address

Diversity information (optional)

If the trainee chose not to share this information during the application process, do not ask for it for the purpose of registering them.
  • Ethnicity
  • Disability

Degrees

If it’s a UK degree

  • Type (for example, BA, BSc or other)
  • Subject
  • Institution
  • Graduation year
  • Grade

If it’s not a UK degree

  • NARIC comparable UK degree (if provided)
  • Subject
  • Country where the degree was obtained
  • Graduation year

Course or programme details

  • Subject
  • Age range
  • Start date
  • End date

Allocations information

If the course has allocated places, you’ll need to know whether the trainee is using an allocated place.
