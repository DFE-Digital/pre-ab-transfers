Assessment Only (AO) route guidance

Assessment Only is a route to Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) in England for:

  • experienced teachers with a degree
  • people with teaching qualifications from other countries

It allows teachers to demonstrate that they already meet all the QTS standards over a period of no more than 12 weeks. They are not considered to be trainees as they already have experience or qualifications.

To find out more details on the criteria that ITT providers need to meet and entry requirements, read AO: criteria and supporting advice.

Registering AO candidates and census

AO candidates do not count towards the ITT census as they are already teaching or have a teaching qualification from another country. This means that there are no deadlines for registering AO candidates.

You can register AO candidates at any stage during the academic year.
